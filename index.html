Bio

Lixiao Cui is a Ph.D. student of Nankai-Baidu Joint Lab, College of Computer Science, Nankai University, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaoguang Liu. He received the B.E. degree from Nankai University in 2019. His research interests mainly focus on storage systems, especially leveraging new hardware / algorithms to enhance the performance and cost-effectiveness of storage systems.

I expect to join the job market in 2025. If you have any job opportunities, please feel free to contact me.
